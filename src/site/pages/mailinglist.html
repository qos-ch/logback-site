Project Mailing Lists

A mailing list is an electronic discussion forum that anyone can subscribe to. When someone sends an email message to the mailing list, a copy of that message is broadcast to everyone who is subscribed to that mailing list.

The "announce" list is reserved for announcing releases. As such, the traffic on this list is guaranteed to be very low.

The logback-dev is the list for the developpers of logback. It is a good forum for asking questions about how logback works, and how it can be used. Logback developers are usually subscribed to this list and may offer support.

Note Only subscribers can post to our mailing lists. If you are subscribed and your posts bounce, make sure that the address you post from matches exactly your subscription address.
